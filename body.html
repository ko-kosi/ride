About Us

Ride Now is Nigeria’s trusted platform for fast, safe, and reliable rides. Our professional drivers and modern vehicles ensure you travel in comfort and style, whether for business, leisure, or daily commutes.

Our mission is to make transportation seamless and enjoyable for everyone in Nigeria. We are committed to providing:

  • 24/7 Customer Support
  • Clean & Comfortable Cars
  • Transparent Pricing
  • Easy Booking Process
  • Experienced & Vetted Drivers
  • Coverage in Major Nigerian Cities
  • Commitment to Safety & Security
  • Multiple Payment Options
  • Regular Vehicle Maintenance
